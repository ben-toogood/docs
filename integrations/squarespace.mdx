---
title: Squarespace
---

In order to connect HutchOS with Squarespace, you will beed to be on the Squarespace Commerce Advanced or Premium plan.

To connect your Squarespace account to HutchOS, follow the below steps:

1. Within the Squarespace dashboard:

- Open the Developer tools panel
- Click Developer API Keys
- Click Generate Key
- Enter `HutchOS` as the Key Name
- Under Permissions, check **Orders** and **Inventory**
- Click Next
- Copy your unique API key

2. Navigate to [Settings > Integrations](https://web.tryhutch.co.uk/clients/settings/integrations) within the HutchOS dashboard, and press **Connect** within the Squarespace section.

<img height="200" src="/images/squarespace-1.png" alt="Step 1" />

3. Enter your API Key in the pop and press **Connect**
