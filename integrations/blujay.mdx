---
title: BluJay (Boots)
---

BluJay, now part of [e2open](https://www.e2open.com/) is the integration patner of Boots.com, allowing brands to fulfil Boots.com orders from 3rd party locations.

To fulfil Boots orders via HutchOS, you need connect your account via Blujay. You will need the following information from your Boots representative:

- supplier_ean
- supplier_code
- sftp_host
- sftp_port
- sftp_username
- sftp_password

Once you have this information, please contact your HutchOS account manager in order to connect the integration.
