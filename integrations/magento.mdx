title: Magento
---

To connect your Magento 2 store to Pimento, follow the below steps:

1. Within your Magento store, go to the navigation bar on the left hand side and press **System**

2. Within the popup, select **Integrations** (within the Extensions category)

3. Add the integration name as Pimento, and enter your user password within the **Current User Identity Verification** section.

<img height="200" src="/images/magento-1.png" alt="Step 1" />

4. On the sidebar is two subtabs, Integration Info and API. Click on **API**.

<img height="200" src="/images/magento-2.png" alt="Step 2" />

5. Select **All** within Resource Access, to give Pimento all the necessary permissions

6. Save the integration and then activate it.

7. Navigate to [Settings > Integrations](https://web.tryhutch.co.uk/clients/settings/integrations) within the Pimento dashboard, and press **Connect** within the Magento section.

<img height="200" src="/images/magento-3.png" alt="Step 3" />

7. Enter the Store URL (e.g. www.mystore.com), along with the API keys which will be visible in the Magento dashboard under **Integration Details**

8. Press connect and your Magento 2 store will be connected to Pimento
