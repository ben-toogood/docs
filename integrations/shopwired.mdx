title: ShopWired
---

To connect your ShopWired account to HutchOS, follow the below steps:

1. Navigate to the **Your Account** section of the ShopWired dashboard. Scroll to the bottom of the page to the **API Access** section.

<img height="200" src="/images/shopwired-1.jpeg" alt="Step 1" />

3. Once you have these credentials, navigate to [Settings > Integrations](https://web.tryhutch.co.uk/clients/settings/integrations) within the HutchOS dashboard, and press **Connect** within the ShopWired section.

<img height="200" src="/images/shopwired-2.png" alt="Step 2" />

4. Once you enter these credentials and then press **Connect**. Your ShopWired store is now connected to HutchOS.
