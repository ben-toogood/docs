title: eBay
---

[eBay](https://www.ebay.co.uk/) is a global e-commerce platform which allows individuals and businesses to sell online.

To connect your eBay account to HutchOS, follow the below steps:

1. Navigate to [Settings > Integrations](https://web.tryhutch.co.uk/clients/settings/integrations) within the HutchOS dashboard, and press **Connect** within the eBay section.

<img height="200" src="/images/ebay-1.png" alt="Step 1" />

2. You will be redirected to eBay where you need to login to your account

<img height="200" src="/images/ebay-2.png" alt="Step 2" />

3. Click **Agree and Continue** when prompted to allow HutchOS access

<img height="200" src="/images/ebay-3.png" alt="Step 3" />

4. You will be redirected back to the HutchOS dashboard and your eBay store is now connected!
