---
title: TikTok
---

To connect your TikTok account to HutchOS, follow the below steps:

1. Navigate to [Settings > Integrations](https://web.tryhutch.co.uk/clients/settings/integrations) within the HutchOS dashboard, and press **Connect** within the TikTok section.

<img height="200" src="/images/tiktok-1.png" alt="Step 1" />

2. You will be redirected to TikTok where you need to confirm your region and then login to your account

<img height="200" src="/images/tiktok-2.png" alt="Step 2" />

3. You will be asked to confirm HutchOS can connect to your TikTok account

4. You will be redirected back to the HutchOS dashboard and your TikTok store is now connected!
