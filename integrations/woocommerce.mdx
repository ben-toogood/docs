---
title: WooCommerce
---

To connect your WooCommerce account to Pimento, follow the below steps:

1. Navigate to **Settings** > **Permalinks** within the Wordpress sidebare. Ensure the Permalink structure is setup to **Day and name**.

<img height="200" src="/images/woocommerce-1.png" alt="Step 1" />

2. Within the **WooCommerce** tab of the sidebar, click on **Settings**

<img height="200" src="/images/woocommerce-2.png" alt="Step 2" />

3. Within **Advanced** > **Legacy API**, ensure the Legacy API is enabled and saved.

<img height="200" src="/images/woocommerce-3.png" alt="Step 3" />

4. Next, go to **Advanced** > **REST API** and press **Create an API Key**

<img height="200" src="/images/woocommerce-4.png" alt="Step 4" />

5. Enter `Pimento` as the description and set permissions to `Read/Write`. Then press **Generate API key**.

<img height="200" src="/images/woocommerce-5.png" alt="Step 5" />

6. You will be provided with a Consumer Key and Consumer Secret. These will be needed in a later step.

<img height="200" src="/images/woocommerce-6.png" alt="Step 6" />

6. Once you have the credentials, navigate to [Settings > Integrations](https://web.tryhutch.co.uk/clients/settings/integrations) within the Pimento dashboard, and press **Connect** within the WooCommerce section.

<img height="200" src="/images/woocommerce-7.png" alt="Step 7" />

7. Enter the Store URL (e.g. `https://www.mystore.com`) along with the API Key and Secret generated in the earlier step.

<img height="200" src="/images/woocommerce-8.png" alt="Step 8" />

8. Press **Connect** and your WooCommerce store will now be connected to Pimento.
