title: Cratejoy
---

[Cratejoy](https://cratejoy.com) is a marketplace specifically designed for subscription boxes.

To connect your Cratejoy account to Pimento, follow the below steps:

1. Navigate to the **API** section of the Cratejoy dashboard, within **Platform Settings**

<img height="200" src="/images/cratejoy-1.png" alt="Step 1" />

2. Click on the green **Generate a new Key** button, a pop-up will appear with the Client ID and Client Secret Key. Save these credentials somewhere safe, as they cannot be accessed again once you leave this page.

<img height="200" src="/images/cratejoy-2.png" alt="Step 2" />

3. Once you have these credentials, navigate to [Settings > Integrations](https://web.tryhutch.co.uk/clients/settings/integrations) within the Pimento dashboard, and press **Connect** within the Cratejoy section.

<img height="200" src="/images/cratejoy-3.png" alt="Step 3" />

4. Enter the API Credentials in the popup and press **Connect**

<img height="200" src="/images/cratejoy-4.png" alt="Step 3" />
