---
title: Shopify
---

To connect your Shopify account to Pimento, follow the below steps:

1. Navigate to the **Settings** section of the Shopify dashboard. Select **Apps and sales channels** from the sidebar.

2. Press the **Develop apps** button in the top right corner

<img height="200" src="/images/shopify-1.png" alt="Step 1" />

3. Press the **Create an app** button in the top right corner

<img height="200" src="/images/shopify-2.png" alt="Step 2" />

4. Enter the App name as `Pimento OS` and then select yourself as the App developer.

<img height="200" src="/images/shopify-3.png" alt="Step 3" />

5. Press **Configure Admin API Scopes**

<img height="200" src="/images/shopify-4.png" alt="Step 4" />

6. Enable the following scopes

- **Products**: Read Access

- **Assigned fulfilment orders**: Read and Write access

- **Fulfilment services**: Read and Write access

- **Inventory**: Read and Write access

- **Locations**: Read access

- **Merchant-managed fulfilment orders**: Read and Write access

- **Orders**: Read and Write access

- **Third-party fulfilment orders**: Read and Write access

- **View or manage fulfillment services**: Read and Write access

<img height="200" src="/images/shopify-5.png" alt="Step 5" />

7. Press **Install app**

<img height="200" src="/images/shopify-6.png" alt="Step 6" />

8. Confirm the popup

<img height="200" src="/images/shopify-7.png" alt="Step 7" />

8. Note down the Access Token, then navigate to [Settings > Integrations](https://web.tryhutch.co.uk/clients/settings/integrations) within the Pimento dashboard, and press **Connect** within the Shopify section.

<img height="200" src="/images/shopify-8.png" alt="Step 8" />

9. Enter your Store Slug, this can be found in the URL bar of the Shopify dashboard

<img height="200" src="/images/shopify-9.png" alt="Step 9" />

10. Enter the Access Token you noted down in the earlier step.

11. Press **Connect**, your Shopify store is now connected to Pimento.
