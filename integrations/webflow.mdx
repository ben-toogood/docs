title: Webflow
---

To connect your Webflow account to HutchOS, follow the below steps:

1. Navigate to the **Settings** section of the Webflow dashboard. Select **Apps & Integrations** from the sidebar

<img height="200" src="/images/webflow-1.png" alt="Step 1" />

2. Scroll down to **API Access** and press **Generate V1 token**

<img height="200" src="/images/webflow-2.png" alt="Step 2" />

3. Select **Generate token** from within the popup. Copy the API Key which is generated.

<img height="200" src="/images/webflow-3.png" alt="Step 3" />

4. Once you have these credentials, navigate to [Settings > Integrations](https://web.tryhutch.co.uk/clients/settings/integrations) within the HutchOS dashboard, and press **Connect** within the Webflow section.

<img height="200" src="/images/webflow-4.png" alt="Step 4" />

5. Enter the API Key you generated in the earlier steps and then press **Connect**. Your Webflow store is now connected to HutchOS.

<img height="200" src="/images/webflow-5.png" alt="Step 5" />
