title: Etsy
---

[Etsy](https://www.etsy.com/) is an marketplace where you can buy and sell unique, handmade, and vintage items from sellers around the world.

To connect your Etsy account to Pimento, follow the below steps:

1. Navigate to [Settings > Integrations](https://web.tryhutch.co.uk/clients/settings/integrations) within the Pimento dashboard, and press **Connect** within the Etsy section.

<img height="200" src="/images/etsy-1.png" alt="Step 1" />

2. You will be redirected to Etsy where you need to login to your account

<img height="200" src="/images/etsy-2.png" alt="Step 2" />

3. Click **Grant access** when prompted to allow Pimento access

<img height="200" src="/images/etsy-3.png" alt="Step 3" />

4. You will be redirected back to the Pimento dashboard and your Etsy store is now connected!
