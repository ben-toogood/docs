---
title: Zoho
---

To connect your Zoho account to Pimento, follow the below steps:

1. Navigate to [Settings > Integrations](https://web.tryhutch.co.uk/clients/settings/integrations) within the Pimento dashboard, and press **Connect** within the Zoho section.

<img height="200" src="/images/zoho-1.png" alt="Step 1" />

2. You will be redirected to Zoho where you need to login to your account

<img height="200" src="/images/zoho-2.png" alt="Step 2" />

3. You will be redirected back to the Pimento dashboard and your Zoho store is now connected!

<img height="200" src="/images/zoho-3.png" alt="Step 3" />
