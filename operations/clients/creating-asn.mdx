Title: Creating an ASN
--

In Pimento, ASN is an acronym for Advance Shipping Notice. This is a inbound shipment that is on its way to your warehouse.
Creating ASNs is important because it give you and your warehouse visability of stock that is coming in and it allows the warehouse to prepair for the inbound shipments.

**Creating a ASN**
Please note: Before you can create a ASN, your products must be uploaded on the system. If you need assistance with creating and uploading your products please refer to the Uploading Inventory doc
