### Supported Services

HutchOS Supports the following DPD Ireland services:

- DPD 2 Day (6)
- DPD Classic Road (1)
- DPD Overnight (1)
- DPD Saturday (2)

### Connecting DPD Ireland

In order to connect your DPD Ireland account to HutchOS, we require the following information:

- Username
- Password
- API Key
