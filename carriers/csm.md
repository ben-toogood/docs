### Supported Services

HutchOS Supports the following CSM services:

- CSM 10:30am (22)
- CSM 12pm (23)
- CSM 24 (24)
- CSM 900 (21)
- CSM DHL Paket (14)
- CSM Economy (26)
- CSM Express (25)
- CSM Saturday (80)
- CSM Saturday By 10:30am (82)
- CSM Saturday By 12pm (81)
- CSM Sunday (83)
- CSM Sunday By 12pm (84)
- CSM eCom Plus (10)
- CSM eCom (09)
- CSM eCom Express (11)

### Connecting CSM

In order to connect your CSM account to HutchOS, we require the following information:

- Username
- Password
- Account Number
