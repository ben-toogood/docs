Inserts can come in many different forms such as stickers, card inserts often known as Thank You Cards, tissue paper or sweets. Anything that is an addition with no money value. Brands will often add a Thank You Card or Instructions Card to orders. It can be automatically set up using Workflows to add this into each order when it comes into HutchOS.
